Specialized UAE Supplier for Industrial Filtration Solutions

Al Nosa Importing Establishment is an Abu Dhabi-based industrial supplier focused on advanced gas turbine air intake filtration solutions for the UAE oil & gas and power generation sectors.

In collaboration with global filtration technology partners, including W. L. Gore & Associates, Al Nosa supports end users with technical coordination, filtration optimization, local logistics, and site support.

Our objective is to help customers improve turbine reliability, reduce compressor fouling, manage differential pressure, and extend filter service life in demanding operating environments.
